Components were designed using SolidWorks. Prototypes were fabricated using CamBam to generate toolpaths in G-code for machining on a CNC mill. The headplate and window frame were milled from aluminum plate. The mold for casting PDMS-based windows was designed in three parts[SC1] . The middle component was milled from PTFE.
